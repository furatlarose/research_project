Introduction to Class platform

Educational websites are considered very important today, as teachers, students, researchers, and thinkers also resort to them for the purpose of knowledge. In web design, as designers, we need to keep up with the latest developments, the most important of which is the entry of artificial intelligence technologies into this field, which in turn contributes to providing many useful and distinctive technologies. In designing this site, artificial intelligence was used, which helps make the site more attractive by displaying content and information to users. Also improving the quality and security of the site, providing the needs and interests of users, and accurately answering any scientific content, whether a question or a book, that is searched for on the site.

Class platform Objectives

  • Saving the user effort and time by searching various sites, as our site provides a set of approved and correct information from reliable sources.
  • The site is not limited to providing information only, but it also provides books that the user can download and read.
  • Enabling the user to quickly and appropriately access the information on the site and use it with ease through the public or private Internet.
  • Providing safety and confidentiality on the site, as user data is kept strictly confidential.
  • We seek to provide information on the site in more than one language so that it is widely spread among countries, so that if anyone enters the site, he will not feel disturbed due to his lack of understanding of the language.
